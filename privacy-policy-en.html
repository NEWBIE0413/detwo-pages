Detwo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

RealMyWorld ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Detwo app ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Apple Sign In Data: Your name and email address as provided through Apple Sign In.
  • Detox Program Data: Detox rules, program duration, and blocked app categories you configure.
  • Usage Records: Your detox program participation history and progress.

Detwo uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls) for app blocking. Specific app usage time data is processed only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and operating the Service (account management, detox program execution)
  • Enabling the companion feature (sharing progress with a partner you invite)
  • Improving the Service and resolving issues

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Your personal information is retained while you use the Service. Upon account deletion, all data is promptly removed. If retention is required by law, data will be stored separately for the legally mandated period.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except:

  • When you invite a companion and consent to sharing progress
  • When required by law

5. Service Providers

We use the following service providers for data processing:

  • Supabase Inc. — Data storage and authentication

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or suspension of processing of your personal information at any time. You can delete your account within the app or by contacting us via email.
