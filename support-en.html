FAQ

Q. Can I unblock apps during a detox program?

Yes, temporary unlocking is available for urgent situations. However, we recommend keeping the block active throughout the program for the best results.

Q. Can I change my companion?

Yes. Go to Settings, disconnect your current companion, and invite a new one.

Q. How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed.

Q. What is the Family Controls permission?

It uses Apple's Screen Time API to block selected apps on your device. This permission works locally on your device only — no usage data is sent to external servers.
